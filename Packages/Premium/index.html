How It Works

A Process
Worthy of the Result

Every Vanguard build follows a deliberate, transparent four-phase process. You'll know exactly where your project stands — and why — at every milestone.

I

Discovery

Site visit, vision alignment, and feasibility. We get to know you and the land before a line is drawn.

II

Design

Full architectural plans, material selections, and 3D renderings. No surprises before ground breaks.

III

Build

Expert trades, weekly updates, and real-time budget tracking. Transparency from frame to finish.

IV

Handover

Final walkthrough, systems orientation, and a 2-year warranty package. The beginning of your home's story.
