Our Story

Three Generations
of Flavor

My grandmother, Mamá Rosa, learned to cook in a small kitchen in Oaxaca. She brought those recipes with her when she came to the US in 1978 — and every event we cater carries a little piece of that kitchen with it.

Today, my family and I serve over 200 events a year across the Indianapolis area, from backyard birthdays to corporate lunches for hundreds. Every dish is made by hand, with the same care Mamá Rosa taught us.

"Food is how we love people. Everything else is just details."
— Elena Morales, Founder
