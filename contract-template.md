## 4. Revision Policy

This agreement includes **_______** round(s) of revisions.

A revision round means: the client reviews the delivered work and submits one consolidated list of changes. PocketSod LLC implements those changes once. This counts as one revision round.

Additional revision rounds beyond what is included are billed at **$75/hour**, minimum 1 hour, and must be approved in writing before work begins.

A revision is a change to existing work. New features or pages added after the project begins are considered out-of-scope and will be quoted separately.
